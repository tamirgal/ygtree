Paula Steinmetz Klein

Born: 1893
Died: 1944    

Comments: Born in Bochco, Marmaros (Romania of today) and moved to Ungvar/Uzhgorod. Worked at the family business and had two hats shops in the town.
In 1944 was sent to Auschwitz with the rest of the jews and never survived.
