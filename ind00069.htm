Yehuda Klein

Born: 1907
Died: 1980    

Comments: Yehuda (Lotzi)  Klein was born in 1907.  His mother died during his delivery,... and  he was raised by his sister Antonia,... When  Antonia got married, he grew  as a brother of the Goldstein sons.
He was an active zionist , in the Hashomer Hatzayir and made Aliya to Israel before World war 2. He lived in kibutz Kfar-Glickson, where he met his wife Rachel Hirsh. She was the eldest sister  of Yacov Hirsh, husband of Tsipora Farkash. Later on they moved to another Kibutz, Usha, near Haifa.
In 1951, they decided to emigrate to  Sydney (Australia), where he died in  1980.
