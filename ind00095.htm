Moshe  Israel Klein

Born: 1890
Died: 1944    

Comments: Born in Ungvar, married to Paula Steinmetz. Worked together with his wife in the hat making business and was a Sales agent.
Was known as Jewish Scholar and could have been a rabbi if he wanted to.
Was sent, together with the other jews of his town to Auschwitz in 1944 and died there with his wife and his small child Gabi.
