Elie  Patan

Born: 29-May-1928

Comments: Elie was born in Cairo, Egypt to Ezra Patan (Abed) and Victoria Sasson.
Married to Mary Abada and immigrated to Israel with his family after the Sinai war , 11/1956. Elie was a modelist and managed the modeling department at "Beged-Or", a worldwide leather clothing manufacturer.
Elie is a self made man. Studied Kabala, History and lately became addicted to computers and photoshop and enjoying his 6 grandchildren.
