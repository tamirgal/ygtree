Moshe Klein

Born: 1855
Died: 1925    

Comments: Moshe Klein was a vineyard expert.
His job was to produce  and improve excellent wines, specially the "Elicant"specie.
Special care and attention were given to Kosher wines,... as many of the clients were jews.
