Asaf Gal

Born: 9-Mar-1977

Comments: Asaf Born in Haifa and raised in Nofit, Israel. He graduated high School and was drafted to the IDF where he served for 3 years as a Sargent at the Engineering Corps.
After the army, like many young Israelis, Asaf went to South America for tracking and touring for almost a year. Then he studied in the Technion, Israel Institue of Technologies and graduated in Electrical engineering as well as in Physics.
He worked for Intel for couple of years and then in 1995 he decided to  study again. This time he joined a direct Doctoral Program of Neural Computation, also known as "Brain Science" in the Hebrew University, Jerusalem.
